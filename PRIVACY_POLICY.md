# Privacy Policy

**AirportWaitTimes**
Last updated: March 29, 2026

## Overview

AirportWaitTimes ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Profile picture URL

### User-Submitted Data
- Wait time reports you voluntarily submit for airports

### Automatically Collected Data
- Basic app usage data for improving the service

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate your account and provide access to the App
- To display your submitted wait time reports to other users
- To determine eligibility for early adopter pricing
- To verify in-app purchases

## Data Storage

- Account information is stored securely on our servers and locally on your device
- We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Sign-In**: For authentication. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Apple StoreKit**: For in-app purchases. Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

## Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by:
- Signing out of the App (clears local data)
- Contacting us at the email below for full server-side data deletion

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
